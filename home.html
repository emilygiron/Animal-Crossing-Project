Welcome to the Animal Crossing Website!

In this website, I will be talking and presenting datasets about a popular video game called Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Specifically, I will be talking about the data behind all 391 villagers of ACNH (before the 2.0 update). Villagers are these cute characters that you can have in your game and they come with many characteristics like different species, gender, birthdays, catchphrases, hobbies, and many more.

With this in mind, I came to the question of who is the most expensive villager? Most animal crossing players pay (using bells or Nook Mile Tickets) for specific villagers based on these characteristics.

The first dataset I used is from kaggle , it is a list of all villagers and their characteristics in Animal Crossing before the 2.0 update.

The second dataset I used is from a popular villager tier list that had the data of actual users inputting what villager they sold for bells. Just a disclaimer that this google spreadsheet is live so as more users input more data, the visuals will not change.
