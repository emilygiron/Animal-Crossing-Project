Species


One characteristic of villagers is that they come in all kinds of species. For example, there are horses, birds, cats, dogs, and many more species like in the real world. So, I wanted to see which species is the most expensive in bells, which is the main currency in the game, and in NMTS (which is less common in the game). On top of that, you are able to see the average bells and NMTs per species based on gender as well. You can also filter based on personality type as well. Each personality type is exclusive to one gender. The male personality type are: smug, lazy, jock, and cranky. The female personality type are: peppy, snooty, normal, and big sister.
